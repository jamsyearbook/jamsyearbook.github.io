Bulletin

    Dear 8th grade parents,
    This year we will continue the tradition of having an 8th-grade baby picture section in the John Adams Yearbook. Baby pictures are a way to show how your child has grown over the years. It can be of any age, preferably 3 years old or younger. If you would like to include your child’s baby picture in our yearbook, please email a .jpeg of the photo to Ms. Goldberg, the yearbook advisor, at Johnadamsybk@gmail.com. If you do not have a digital image of your photo, please bring a picture in a sealed envelope with your child’s name, advisory teacher, and ID number into Ms. Goldberg in room 34 to be scanned. Please submit your photos
